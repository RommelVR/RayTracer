RayTracer
=========

A Haskell implementation of Paul Heckbert's business card raytracer.

## How to build?
If you don't want to be waiting for several minutes, your best bet is to ensure `-O` flag is enabled when compiling.

`ghc -O Tracer.hs`

## How to run?

`./Tracer > output.ppm`
